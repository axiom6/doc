[#DomainAbstract]
Viable Propositions are promoted to Value Propositions by Discovery and their parts
categorized into Entitle, Portfolio and Acquire.

// image::graph/Product.png[alt="Product",align="center"]

[#DomainDescription]
[horizontal]
Entitle::     The secure allocation of resources by roles.
Propose::     The proposed ideas for new products.
Acquire::     A collected written, drawn and presented representation of thought.
Portfolio::   The entire bundle of current and future products that a company offers.
