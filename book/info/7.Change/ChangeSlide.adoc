== [.black]#Change#

Initially conducts Tests to ascertain deployment readiness, mitigated by Transition and backed by repeatable automation to insure successful delivery to production.

image::pnh2/Change.png[alt="Change",align="center"]

[horizontal]
Test::       Quality, defect and risk measures at many levels and types.
Transition:: Addresses new and updated deployments and disruptions.
Deploy::     Continously noves new component to Deliver.
Maintain::   Insures that Deliver is running smoothly.
