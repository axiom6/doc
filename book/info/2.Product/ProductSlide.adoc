== [.black]#Product#

Viable Propositions are promoted to Value Propositions by Discovery and their parts categorized into Entitlement, Portfolio and Intelligence.

image::pnh2/Product.png[alt="Product",align="center"]

[horizontal]
Proposition:: The proposed ideas for new products.
Entitlement:: The secure allocation of resources by roles.
Portfolio::   The entire bundle of current and future products that a company offers.
Document::    A collected written, drawn and presented representation of thought.
