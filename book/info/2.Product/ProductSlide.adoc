== [.black]#Product#

Viable Propositions are promoted to Value Propositions by Discovery and their parts categorized into Entitlement, Portfolio and Intelligence.

image::Product.svg[alt="Product",align="center"]

[horizontal]
Proposition:: The proposed ideas for new products.
Entitlement:: The secure UI allocation of Resources* to teams by roles.
Portfolio:: The entire bundle of products that a company will offer.
Intelligence:: Data with business descriptions.
